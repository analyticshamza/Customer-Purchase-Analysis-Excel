## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer purchase data for a US-based retail company (RetailMax) to understand revenue performance across product categories and regions using Microsoft Excel.

---

## Objectives
- Identify highest and lowest revenue-generating sales
- Analyze revenue by product category
- Analyze revenue by region
- Ensure data accuracy through proper cleaning

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Techniques Applied
- Data Cleaning (Remove Duplicates)
- Sorting (Revenue, Product Category, Region)
- Filtering (Region and Product Category)
- Subtotals (Revenue aggregation)
- Grouping using Excel Outline

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Sorting and Filtering
- Sorted revenue to identify highest and lowest sales
- Applied filters to analyze specific regions and product categories

### 2. Revenue by Product Category
- Data sorted by Product Category
- Subtotals applied to calculate total revenue per category
- Grouping enabled using Excel Outline

### 3. Revenue by Region
- Data sorted by Region
- Subtotals applied to calculate total revenue per region

---

## Key Insights
- Books generated the highest total revenue
- Clothing and Home Decor were strong contributors
- Electronics generated comparatively lower revenue
- Clear regional revenue differences were observed
